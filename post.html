From Zero to CreateArc: My Mission to Build Without Limits

By Vivek Raj • Published on 29 June 2025

I’m not from an IT background. I don’t have a tech degree or a team behind me. What I do have is ambition, belief, and fire. I’ve been told a hundred times — "Coding nahi aati toh startup kaise banega?", "Pehle job le lo, fir sochna", "Investors ko kya doge?" But I didn’t stop.

**CreateArc** is my answer to all of that. It’s a tool for people like me — jo job nahi, impact banana chahte hain. Jo limited background ke bawajood, unlimited ideas ke saath duniya ke saamne aana chahte hain. CreateArc helps users make powerful business documents and pitch decks, even if they don’t know design or tech.

Main ek simple vision se chala: Har insaan ka idea keemti hai — use duniya ke samne rakhna mushkil nahi hona chahiye. Isliye 80-slide ka pitch deck banaya, sab kuch khud likha, design kiya, structure kiya — bina kisi agency ke. That's CreateArc: a platform for creators, hustlers, and idea warriors.

“When you don’t have the privilege to take shortcuts, you build the long way — brick by brick.”

Abhi toh bas shuruaat hai. Main ek community banana chahta hoon jo sapne ke saath system bhi banaye. Jaha log dare nahi, dare to build.

– Vivek Raj
